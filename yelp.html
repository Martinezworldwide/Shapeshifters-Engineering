OSINT Tools

Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) tools play a critical role in gathering actionable insights for threat hunting. Here are some of the top OSINT tools:

  • Maltego: Visualize connections between data points for deeper investigations.
  • Shodan: Discover vulnerabilities in connected devices and systems.
  • Have I Been Pwned: Check if email addresses or data have been exposed in breaches.
  • Social Searcher: Track mentions on social media for threat insights.
