Innovation in Acoustic Mechanics

Acoustic mechanics focuses on the science of sound transmission without relying on electronic amplification. My work delves into the integration of textiles with acoustic properties to create sustainable, non-electrical sound solutions for diverse applications.

String-Based Audio Solutions

String-based audio solutions explore the use of physical vibrations transmitted through strings or fibers to produce sound. These systems can be used in non-electrical headphones and other devices, offering sustainable alternatives to traditional audio technology.
